Mood Help

Below are more detailed descriptions of our limited list of moods.

1. Calm -- not showing any emotions
2. Happy -- feeling or showing pleasure or contentment
3. Silly -- foolish, goofy, laughing
4. Relaxed -- free from tension and anxiety, at ease
5. Nervous -- easily agitated or alarmed, anxious, high strung
6. Annoyed -- irritated, bothered
7. Sad -- unhappy, feeling or showing sorrow
8. Shy -- reserved, timid
9. Surprised -- caught off guard, shocked
10. Hungry -- wanting food
11. Angry -- feeling and showing hostility and displeasure
12. Confused -- bewildered, unable to think clearly
13. Sleepy -- tired, needing sleep
14. Sick -- unwell, under the weather
15. Hurt -- injured, in pain
16. Hot -- warm, sweating
